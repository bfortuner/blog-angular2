Needlessly wordy conspectus about myself

This author grew up in the small colonial town of Litchfield, Connecticut. A number of uneventful things happened there. He left shortly after to attend the University of Virginia where he accumulated credits in cognitively undemanding subjects like business and philosophy. He also sang in an a capella group, wrote for a humor publication, frequented a debating society, and from time to time attended class. Despite his greatest desire to remain at the university forever, a minor administrative oversight involving the incorrect rounding of floats gave him one credit too many and he was promptly graduated. Keen not to stay on his parents' futon forever, he found inspiration in the classic movie “Sleepless in Seattle” and decided to move there where he still lives, works, and gets wet.
